***Summary***
This was the resume that I was able to use to apply to my first engineering job at Vast. Admittedly the hiring process was more based on a group interview, and I am not sure how much my resume factored into the choice they made. I think it was the networking after the interview that mattered more. I remember showing my machine learning projects to the CTO/Chief Data Scientist of the company Prof. David Franke, and him being interested in what I did. I think it was that interaction that really got my foot in the door.
